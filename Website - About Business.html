What is this business about?


I consider myself one of the best cooks and chefs at home.
Every time we have visitors at our house, they love the food
that I prepare. Because of the encouragement that they
gave me to sell food, I started baking products like breads
and cakes, then homemade food. Every day, I gain new
clients through word of mouth.

My goal for the business is to provide my customers a good
taste of Filipino food. At Mommy Lyn's Kitchen, we take great
pleasure in creating mouthwatering foods that amuse your
taste buds and leave you wanting more. We have you covered
whether you're organizing a little group gathering or just
looking for a delectable lunch. As for the future, my big to
let the business grow even further and open my own
restaurant soon.
